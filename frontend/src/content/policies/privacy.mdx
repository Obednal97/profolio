## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures including:

**Data Encryption**

- TLS 1.3 for all data transmission
- AES-256-GCM for data at rest
- Encrypted database fields for sensitive data
- Hardware security module for key management

**Access Controls**

- Multi-factor authentication (2FA/MFA)
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- IP allowlisting for admin access
- Session timeout and device management

**Infrastructure Security**

- SOC 2 Type II certified data centres
- Network segregation and firewalls
- DDoS protection
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

**Monitoring and Response**

- 24/7 security monitoring
- Intrusion detection systems
- Automated threat response
- Incident response team

### 6.2 Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach:

1. We will contain and investigate immediately
2. Notify affected users within 72 hours
3. Report to the ICO as required
4. Provide guidance on protective measures
5. Conduct post-incident review and improvements

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 UK GDPR Rights

As a UK resident, you have the right to:

**Access Your Data**

- Request a copy of all personal data we hold
- Receive data in a structured, machine-readable format
- Understand how your data is processed

**Rectification**

- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Complete incomplete personal data
- Update outdated information

**Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**

- Request deletion of your personal data
- Remove data no longer necessary for processing
- Withdraw consent for data processing

**Restriction of Processing**

- Limit how we use your data
- Suspend processing while disputes are resolved
- Maintain data without further processing

**Data Portability**

- Export your data in standard formats
- Transfer data to another service
- Receive machine-readable copies

**Object to Processing**

- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Object to legitimate interest processing
- Refuse automated decision-making

### 7.2 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

1. Email privacy@profolio.app with your request
2. We will verify your identity for security
3. Respond within 30 days of verification
4. No fee unless requests are excessive
5. Right to appeal to the ICO if unsatisfied

### 7.3 Account Controls

You can directly control your data through:

- Account settings dashboard
- Privacy preferences centre
- Data export tools
- Account deletion option
- API connection management
- Communication preferences

## 9. Data Retention

### 9.1 Retention Periods

We retain your data for:

**Active Accounts**

- Personal data: Duration of account plus 30 days
- Financial data: 7 years for tax purposes
- Transaction logs: 5 years for audit trail
- Support tickets: 2 years after resolution

**Closed Accounts**

- Account marked for deletion immediately
- Personal data anonymised within 30 days
- Legal compliance data retained as required
- Backups purged within 90 days

**Marketing Data**

- Consent records: 3 years or until withdrawn
- Email engagement: 2 years of inactivity
- Unsubscribe records: Permanently retained

### 9.2 Retention Criteria

We consider:

- Legal and regulatory requirements
- Limitation periods for legal claims
- Tax and accounting obligations
- Legitimate business needs
- User instructions and preferences
