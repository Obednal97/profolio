# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** December 1, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Profolio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our portfolio management platform and related services (collectively, the "Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password, and profile details
- **Financial Data:** Portfolio holdings, transaction history, asset values
- **API Credentials:** Encrypted third-party service credentials (e.g., Trading 212)
- **Communications:** Messages, support requests, and feedback

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Performance Data:** Error logs, response times, system metrics
- **Security Logs:** Login attempts, security events, access patterns

### 2.3 Third-Party Data

- **Market Data:** Real-time financial data from authorized providers
- **API Data:** Portfolio and transaction data from connected financial services

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes

- **Service Provision:** Manage your portfolio, process transactions, display data
- **Account Management:** Create and maintain your account, authenticate access
- **Security:** Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats
- **Communication:** Send important updates, notifications, and support responses

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes

- **Analytics:** Improve service performance and user experience
- **Development:** Develop new features and enhance existing functionality
- **Legal Compliance:** Meet regulatory requirements and legal obligations

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 4.2 Limited Sharing Circumstances

We may share your information only in these situations:

- **Service Providers:** Trusted partners who help operate our platform (hosting, analytics)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or regulatory request
- **Business Protection:** To protect our rights, property, or safety
- **Consent:** When you explicitly authorize sharing
- **Business Transfer:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

### 4.3 Third-Party Services

- **Financial APIs:** We securely transmit encrypted credentials to authorized financial services
- **Analytics:** We use privacy-focused analytics that don't track individual users
- **Infrastructure:** Our hosting providers have strict data protection agreements

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted and stored using industry-standard encryption
- **API Security:** Financial credentials encrypted with AES-256-GCM
- **Access Controls:** Multi-factor authentication and role-based access
- **Monitoring:** Continuous security monitoring and threat detection

### 5.2 Data Storage

- **Location:** Data stored in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centers
- **Backup:** Regular encrypted backups with geographic redundancy
- **Retention:** Data retained only as long as necessary for service provision

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 Access and Control

- **View Data:** Access all personal data we have about you
- **Update Information:** Modify or correct your account information
- **Download Data:** Export your portfolio data in standard formats
- **Delete Account:** Request complete account and data deletion

### 6.2 Privacy Preferences

- **Communication:** Opt out of non-essential communications
- **Analytics:** Disable optional usage analytics
- **API Connections:** Manage and revoke third-party integrations

### 6.3 Regional Rights

**European Users (GDPR):**
- Right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data
- Right to object to processing and request restrictions
- Right to withdraw consent and file complaints

**California Users (CCPA):**
- Right to know what data is collected and how it's used
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

### 7.1 Cookie Usage

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for login, security, and core functionality
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand usage patterns (opt-out available)
- **Preference Cookies:** Remember your settings and preferences

### 7.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1 Retention Periods

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active and for legitimate business needs
- **Inactive Accounts:** Data deleted after 3 years of inactivity (with prior notice)
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data retained longer for compliance purposes

### 8.2 Deletion Process

When you delete your account:
1. Personal data is immediately marked for deletion
2. Data is anonymized or deleted within 30 days
3. Backups are purged within 90 days
4. Some metadata may be retained for security and compliance

## 9. International Data Transfers

### 9.1 Transfer Mechanisms

When we transfer data internationally, we use:
- **Adequacy Decisions:** Transfers to countries with adequate protection
- **Standard Contractual Clauses:** EU-approved data transfer agreements
- **Binding Corporate Rules:** Internal policies ensuring consistent protection

### 9.2 Safeguards

All international transfers include appropriate safeguards to protect your data rights and freedoms.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected a child's information, we will delete it immediately.
