## Definitions

To help you understand this policy, here are some key terms:

- **"Cookies"** are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website that help us recognise you and remember your preferences
- **"Session Cookies"** are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
- **"Persistent Cookies"** remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
- **"First-party Cookies"** are set by Profolio directly
- **"Third-party Cookies"** are set by external services we use
- **"Strictly Necessary Cookies"** are essential for the website to function
- **"Performance Cookies"** help us understand how visitors use our website
- **"Functionality Cookies"** remember your preferences and choices

## Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

1. **Enable Core Functionality** - Allow you to log in, navigate, and use essential features
2. **Remember Your Preferences** - Store your settings, theme choice, and dashboard layout
3. **Improve Performance** - Understand how our platform is used and optimise accordingly
4. **Enhance Security** - Protect against fraud and unauthorised access
5. **Provide Analytics** - Analyse usage patterns to improve our services

When you connect external financial accounts:

- **Trading 212**: Session management for API connections
- **Market Data Services**: Real-time price data caching
- **Banking APIs**: Secure authentication tokens

These cookies are essential for:

- Maintaining secure API connections
- Synchronising your portfolio data
- Fetching real-time market prices

### Infrastructure Services

Our platform infrastructure may use cookies for:

- **Content Delivery Networks (CDN)**: Optimising content delivery
- **Cloud Services**: Load balancing and server routing
- **Security Services**: DDoS protection and threat detection

### Analytics Partners

We use privacy-focused analytics that:

- Do not track individual users
- Aggregate data anonymously
- Respect Do Not Track settings
- Are GDPR compliant

## Managing Your Cookies

### Cookie Preferences Centre

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

1. Click the cookie icon in the footer
2. Or go to **Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Cookie Preferences**
3. Toggle each category on or off
4. Click "Save Preferences"

### Browser Cookie Management

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

**Google Chrome**

- Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy and security ‚Üí Cookies and other site data
- Choose your preferred cookie settings
- [Chrome Cookie Settings](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)

**Mozilla Firefox**

- Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Cookies and Site Data
- Select your tracking protection level
- [Firefox Cookie Settings](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)

**Safari**

- Go to Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Manage Website Data
- Choose when to block cookies
- [Safari Cookie Settings](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac)

**Microsoft Edge**

- Go to Settings ‚Üí Cookies and site permissions
- Choose your tracking prevention level
- [Edge Cookie Settings](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

**Note:** Blocking all cookies will prevent you from using certain features of our platform, including the ability to log in.

## Other Tracking Technologies

We use browser local storage to:

### Session Storage

We use session storage for:

For security purposes only, we may collect:

## Your Rights and Choices

### Consent

- You can withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time
- Essential cookies cannot be disabled while using the platform
- Your choices are saved and respected across all devices when logged in

### Do Not Track

We honour Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals:

- When enabled, analytics cookies are automatically disabled
- Performance monitoring is limited to essential metrics
- Your privacy choice is respected

### Data Access

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

- Request information about cookies we've set
- Access data collected through cookies
- Request deletion of cookie-related data
- Export your cookie preferences
