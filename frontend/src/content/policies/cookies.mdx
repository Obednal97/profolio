# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** December 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** December 1, 2024

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your experience more convenient and personalized.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

Profolio uses cookies and similar technologies to:
- Keep you logged in securely
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Provide personalized features
- Analyze how our platform is used
- Improve performance and functionality

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies

**Purpose:** Required for basic platform functionality  
**Duration:** Session or up to 1 year  
**Can be disabled:** No (platform won't work properly)

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_token` | User authentication | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Security protection | Session |
| `user_preferences` | Interface settings | 1 year |

### 3.2 Functional Cookies

**Purpose:** Enhanced user experience and features  
**Duration:** Up to 1 year  
**Can be disabled:** Yes (some features may not work)

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `theme_preference` | Dark/light mode setting | 1 year |
| `language_setting` | Preferred language | 1 year |
| `dashboard_layout` | Customized dashboard | 6 months |

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies

**Purpose:** Understanding usage patterns and improving the platform  
**Duration:** Up to 2 years  
**Can be disabled:** Yes (via cookie preferences)

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `analytics_id` | Anonymous usage tracking | 2 years |
| `page_views` | Popular features analysis | 1 year |
| `performance_metrics` | Platform optimization | 6 months |

### 3.4 Security Cookies

**Purpose:** Fraud prevention and account protection  
**Duration:** Session to 30 days  
**Can be disabled:** No (security would be compromised)

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `login_attempts` | Brute force protection | 30 days |
| `device_fingerprint` | Device recognition | 6 months |
| `security_flags` | Suspicious activity detection | Session |

### 4.1 Financial Data Providers

- **Purpose:** Market data and portfolio information
- **Providers:** Trading 212 API, market data services
- **Control:** Managed through your API connections

### 4.2 Infrastructure Services

- **Purpose:** Platform hosting and performance
- **Providers:** Cloud hosting services, CDN providers

### 4.3 Analytics Services

- **Purpose:** Privacy-focused usage analytics
- **Providers:** Self-hosted analytics solutions
- **Data:** Anonymized and aggregated only

## 5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 5.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit Profolio, you'll see a cookie consent banner with options to:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize your preferences

### 5.2 Cookie Preference Center

Access your cookie settings at any time:
1. Go to Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences
2. Toggle categories on/off as desired
3. Save your preferences

### 5.3 Browser Controls

You can also manage cookies through your browser:

**Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preferred setting
3. Manage exceptions for specific sites

**Firefox:**
1. Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Choose standard, strict, or custom settings
3. Manage exceptions as needed

**Safari:**
1. Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
2. Choose cookie blocking preferences
3. Remove stored data if desired

**Edge:**
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
2. Choose your blocking level
3. Manage site-specific permissions

## 6. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use:

### 6.1 Local Storage

- **Purpose:** Temporary data caching and offline functionality
- **Content:** User interface state, temporary calculations
- **Management:** Cleared when you clear browser data

### 6.2 Session Storage

- **Purpose:** Temporary session data
- **Content:** Form data, navigation state
- **Duration:** Cleared when tab is closed

### 6.3 IndexedDB

- **Purpose:** Offline portfolio data caching
- **Content:** Portfolio snapshots, market data cache
- **Management:** Cleared with cookie preferences

## 7. Do Not Track Signals

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser settings:
- When DNT is enabled, we disable non-essential tracking
- Analytics cookies are automatically blocked
- Essential cookies for functionality remain active

## 8. Cookie Retention Periods

| Cookie Type | Retention Period | Automatic Deletion |
|-------------|------------------|-------------------|
| Session | Browser session | When browser closes |
| Authentication | 30 days | After logout or expiry |
| Preferences | 1 year | User can clear anytime |
| Analytics | 2 years | Automatic cleanup |

### 9.2 Consent Management

## 12. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly:
- Set cookies for minors
- Collect data from children
- Track underage users

If we discover cookies from minors, they are immediately deleted.
