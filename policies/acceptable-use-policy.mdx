# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** December 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** December 1, 2024

## 1. Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the guidelines for using Profolio's portfolio management platform. By using our Service, you agree to comply with these guidelines and our Terms of Service.

## 2. Acceptable Uses

### 2.1 Personal Portfolio Management

✅ **Permitted Activities:**
- Track your personal investment portfolios
- Analyze your asset allocation and performance
- Connect your own financial service accounts
- Monitor real estate and alternative investments
- Create reports for personal financial planning
- Export your own data for backup or analysis

### 2.2 Professional Use

✅ **Permitted Activities:**
- Manage client portfolios (with proper authorization)
- Generate reports for financial advisory services
- Use data for investment research and analysis
- Educational demonstrations and training
- Academic research (with appropriate disclosures)

### 2.3 Business Applications

✅ **Permitted Activities:**
- Corporate treasury management
- Pension fund portfolio tracking
- Investment club management
- Family office applications
- Compliance reporting and documentation

## 3. Prohibited Activities

### 3.1 Illegal Activities

❌ **Strictly Prohibited:**
- Money laundering or terrorist financing
- Tax evasion or fraudulent reporting
- Insider trading or market manipulation
- Ponzi schemes or investment fraud
- Violation of financial regulations
- Circumventing sanctions or embargoes

### 3.2 Unauthorized Access

❌ **Strictly Prohibited:**
- Accessing accounts that don't belong to you
- Using someone else's API credentials without permission
- Sharing login credentials with unauthorized persons
- Attempting to bypass security measures
- Reverse engineering or decompiling the platform
- Penetration testing without written permission

### 3.3 System Abuse

❌ **Strictly Prohibited:**
- Overloading servers with excessive requests
- Attempting denial-of-service attacks
- Uploading malicious code or viruses
- Interfering with other users' service
- Creating fake accounts or identities
- Scraping data beyond reasonable personal use

### 3.4 Data Misuse

❌ **Strictly Prohibited:**
- Selling or distributing user data
- Using data for unauthorized marketing
- Sharing sensitive financial information without consent
- Creating profiles of other users
- Violating privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Using data for discriminatory purposes

## 4. API Usage Guidelines

### 4.1 Responsible API Use

✅ **Best Practices:**
- Respect rate limits and usage quotas
- Use API connections only for legitimate purposes
- Keep API credentials secure and private
- Monitor for unusual activity or errors
- Comply with third-party service provider terms

### 4.2 API Restrictions

❌ **Prohibited API Activities:**
- Exceeding documented rate limits
- Using APIs for high-frequency trading (unless authorized)
- Sharing API access with unauthorized parties
- Using APIs to extract competitive intelligence
- Violating broker or exchange terms of service

## 5. Content and Communication

### 5.1 User-Generated Content

✅ **Acceptable Content:**
- Personal financial notes and annotations
- Portfolio descriptions and investment themes
- Educational content and research sharing
- Constructive feedback and feature requests

### 5.2 Prohibited Content

❌ **Unacceptable Content:**
- Investment advice without proper licensing
- Spam, promotional content, or advertisements
- Offensive, discriminatory, or harassing material
- Copyrighted content without permission
- Misleading or fraudulent information
- Content that violates local laws

## 6. Security Responsibilities

### 6.1 Account Security

**Your Responsibilities:**
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication when available
- Keep your contact information current
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- Log out from shared or public computers
- Regularly review account activity

### 6.2 Data Protection

**Your Responsibilities:**
- Protect your own financial data
- Don't share sensitive information in unsecured communications
- Use the platform's security features appropriately
- Report potential security vulnerabilities responsibly
- Follow data protection laws in your jurisdiction

## 7. Compliance Requirements

### 7.1 Financial Regulations

Users must comply with:
- Local securities laws and regulations
- Anti-money laundering (AML) requirements
- Know Your Customer (KYC) obligations
- Tax reporting and disclosure requirements
- Investment advisor regulations (if applicable)

### 7.2 Data Protection Laws

Users must comply with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Other applicable privacy laws
- Cross-border data transfer restrictions

## 8. Monitoring and Enforcement

### 8.1 Platform Monitoring

We may monitor the platform to:
- Detect security threats and abuse
- Ensure compliance with this AUP
- Improve service performance and reliability
- Investigate user reports and complaints
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

### 8.2 Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in:
- **Warning:** Notice of policy violation
- **Feature Restriction:** Limited access to certain features
- **Account Suspension:** Temporary account deactivation
- **Account Termination:** Permanent account closure
- **Legal Action:** Reporting to authorities if required

### 8.3 Appeals Process

If you believe an enforcement action was incorrect:
1. Contact our support team within 30 days
2. Provide detailed explanation and evidence
3. We'll review your case within 5 business days
4. You'll receive a written response with our decision

## 9. Reporting Violations

### 9.1 How to Report

If you observe violations of this AUP:

**Email:** abuse@profolio.app  
**Subject:** AUP Violation Report  
**Include:** Details of the violation, evidence, and your contact information

### 9.2 What We Need

For effective investigation, please provide:
- Clear description of the violation
- Screenshots or other evidence (if available)
- Date and time of the incident
- Your relationship to the situation
- Any relevant account information

### 9.3 Our Response

We will:
- Acknowledge your report within 24 hours
- Investigate the claim thoroughly
- Take appropriate action if violations are confirmed
- Protect your identity as the reporter
- Follow up if additional information is needed

## 10. Self-Hosted Installations

### 10.1 Additional Responsibilities

For self-hosted deployments, you are also responsible for:
- Implementing appropriate security measures
- Complying with data protection laws
- Ensuring proper backup and disaster recovery
- Managing user access and permissions
- Keeping software updated and secure

### 10.2 Support Limitations

For self-hosted installations:
- You are responsible for system administration
- Community support is available through GitHub
- We cannot guarantee security of your implementation
- Professional support may be available separately

## 11. Updates to This Policy

### 11.1 Policy Changes

We may update this AUP to:
- Address new types of abuse
- Comply with legal changes
- Improve platform security
- Clarify existing guidelines

### 11.2 Notification

We will notify you of changes through:
- Email to your registered address
- In-app notifications
- Updates on our website
- GitHub notifications (for open source users)

For questions about this Acceptable Use Policy:

**General Questions:** support@profolio.app  
**AUP Violations:** abuse@profolio.app  
**Legal Matters:** legal@profolio.app  
**Security Issues:** security@profolio.app

## Summary

This Acceptable Use Policy helps maintain a safe, secure, and compliant environment for all Profolio users. Key points:

**✅ Do:**
- Use the platform for legitimate portfolio management
- Protect your account and data
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Report violations and security issues
- Respect other users and the platform

**❌ Don't:**
- Use the platform for illegal activities
- Access accounts that aren't yours
- Abuse the system or APIs
- Share sensitive data inappropriately
- Violate financial or privacy regulations

By following these guidelines, you help us maintain a trustworthy platform for everyone. If you have questions about what's acceptable, please contact us at support@profolio.app.
